================================================================================
QUICK REFERENCE: Files to Transfer to Jetson
================================================================================

Copy these 4 files to your Jetson Orin Nano:

1. models/caption_poc.onnx               (798KB)
2. models/caption_poc_config.json        (141B)
3. data_full/tokenizer.json              (~700KB)
4. jetson_webcam_demo.py                 (16KB)

Total: ~1.5MB

================================================================================
Transfer Command (via SCP)
================================================================================

# Replace <jetson-ip> with your Jetson's IP address
# Replace <username> with your Jetson username (default: jetson)

scp models/caption_poc.onnx <username>@<jetson-ip>:~/diffusion-demo/
scp models/caption_poc_config.json <username>@<jetson-ip>:~/diffusion-demo/
scp data_full/tokenizer.json <username>@<jetson-ip>:~/diffusion-demo/
scp jetson_webcam_demo.py <username>@<jetson-ip>:~/diffusion-demo/

================================================================================
Run Command (on Jetson)
================================================================================

cd ~/diffusion-demo

python3 jetson_webcam_demo.py \
    --onnx caption_poc.onnx \
    --config caption_poc_config.json \
    --tokenizer tokenizer.json \
    --max_len 12 \
    --steps 25

Press SPACEBAR to capture and generate caption!
Press 'q' to quit.
